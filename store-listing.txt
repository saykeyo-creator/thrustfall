APP NAME: Thrustfall

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars max):
Cave combat with physics, weapons, and online PVP. Fly, fight, survive.

FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max):
Thrustfall is a fast-paced 2D cave combat game where you pilot a spaceship through procedurally generated caverns, battling enemies with physics-based flight and an arsenal of devastating weapons.

★ CORE GAMEPLAY
Master gravity-defying flight with precise thrust and rotation controls. Land on bases to heal. Collect weapon pickups scattered across the map. Every cave is different — procedurally generated terrain with seeded randomness ensures fair, unpredictable arenas.

★ 9 WEAPON TYPES
Stock rapid-fire, Spread shot, Rapid twin cannons, Heavy piercing rounds, Laser beam, Burst shotgun, Homing missiles, Shield pickup, and the EMP pulse — a devastating area-of-effect weapon that disables enemy thrust, weapons, and shields.

★ ONLINE PVP MULTIPLAYER
Create or join rooms for 2–8 player battles. The server runs authoritative physics at 60fps — no cheating, no lag exploits. Public matchmaking or private rooms with 4-character codes. Choose your loadout and cosmetics before battle.

★ SURVIVAL MODE
Fight waves of increasingly dangerous bots. Four bot types — Normal, Fast, Tank, and Sniper — each with unique stats. Boss waves every 5th round. Wave modifiers like Low Gravity and Heavy Weapons keep each run fresh. How far can you survive?

★ DEEP PROGRESSION
Earn XP from kills, wins, landings, and pickups. Level up to unlock 6 perks with distinct solo and PVP effects: Reinforced Shield, Quick Loader, Boost Jets, Thick Hull, Scavenger, and Quick Respawn. Build loadouts within a 3-point budget.

★ 14 ACHIEVEMENTS & DAILY CHALLENGES
Track milestones from First Blood to Unstoppable. Complete rotating daily challenges for bonus XP — get homing kills, survive without dying, collect powerups, and more.

★ MASSIVE COSMETIC COLLECTION
• 11 unique ship skins with custom silhouettes
• 6 trail effects (ice, fire, plasma, rainbow, toxic)
• 6 engine sounds (rumble, whine, pulse, roar, hum)
• 6 kill effects (vortex, electric, shatter, nova, void)
All cosmetics are visible to other players in multiplayer.

★ 6 HANDCRAFTED MAPS
The Caves, Deep Canyon, Asteroid Field (low gravity), Twin Fortress, The Labyrinth, and The Arena (open dogfight). Each with unique dimensions and terrain generation.

★ ADAPTIVE MUSIC
A 4-layer dynamic soundtrack responds to combat intensity — from ambient drones to full warzone chaos with war horns and 32nd-note kicks. Heartbeat pulses when health is low. All audio generated in real-time — no audio files, pure synthesis.

★ PRACTICE MODE
Fly solo against bots in any map with your full loadout. Perfect for learning controls and testing perk combinations.

No ads. No pay-to-win. Pure skill-based combat.
